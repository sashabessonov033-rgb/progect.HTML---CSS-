Words can't express how much I love and appreciate you. Sometimes I feel like a madman who's head over heels in love with you. And I want to live with you just as much and continue our relationship.

Even though we may get upset at each other sometimes, that doesn't change the fact that I love you and want to be with you.

I often imagine us lying together in hammocks in our house by the sea (in Spain) in the future, loving each other just as much and even more than we do now.

There are moments when you don't see me looking at you with such tenderness, and in those moments I realize how much I want to be with you and that you are the one I want to spend this life and my future with. Of course, I hope the feeling is mutual.
